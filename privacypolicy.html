Privacy Policy

A Flight Simulation Community Project

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you join 6E Virtual, we may collect the following information:

  • Discord username and ID (for community access)
  • vAMSYS pilot profile data (username, flight records, statistics)
  • SimBrief username (if you choose to link it)

We do not collect personal information such as real names, email addresses, or physical addresses unless voluntarily provided.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Managing your virtual airline pilot account
  • Tracking flight hours, PIREPs, and rank progression
  • Displaying public statistics and leaderboards
  • Community communication via Discord

3. Third-Party Services

6E Virtual uses the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • vAMSYS — Virtual airline management platform
  • Discord — Community communication
  • SimBrief — Flight planning (optional)

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these services. We are not responsible for their data handling practices.

4. Data Storage & Security

Flight data and statistics are stored on vAMSYS servers. We do not independently store copies of your personal data outside of the vAMSYS platform.

We take reasonable measures to protect information within our control but cannot guarantee absolute security of any data transmitted over the internet.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use minimal cookies for theme preference (light/dark mode). We do not use third-party tracking or analytics cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your pilot account and associated data at any time by contacting us through our Discord server. Upon account deletion, your flight records stored on vAMSYS will be handled according to vAMSYS's data retention policies.

7. Children's Privacy

6E Virtual does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us via Discord.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of 6E Virtual after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please reach out through our Discord server.
